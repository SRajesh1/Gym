Fitness Training Roadmap

A structured journey from absolute beginner to elite athlete.

0-3 Months

Phase 1: Beginner

Level Overview

  • Focus: Form, Habit, Mobility
  • Frequency: 3-4 days/week
  • Goal: Muscle activation, Basic strength

Weekly Split

Full Body Split

  • Day 1: Full Body
  • Day 2: Rest / Cardio
  • Day 3: Full Body
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Full Body

Diet Guidelines

  • Protein: Eggs, Milk, Dal, Chicken, Paneer
  • Carbs: Rice, Oats, Fruits
  • Water: 3+ liters/day
  • Avoid junk and sugary drinks

Progress Criteria

  • Master exercise form
  • No excessive soreness
  • Consistent for 8-12 weeks
6-18 Months

Phase 2: Intermediate

Level Overview

  • Focus: Muscle Growth & Strength
  • Frequency: 4-5 days/week
  • Goal: Hypertrophy & Conditioning

Weekly Split

Push-Pull-Legs (PPL)

  • Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulder, Triceps)
  • Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4: Rest / Core
  • Day 5: Upper Body
