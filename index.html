Diet Plans

Fuel your body based on your goals.

Fat Burner

Mass Builder

  • Breakfast: 4 Eggs, toast, peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Steak, rice, and mixed veggies.
  • Dinner: Salmon, sweet potato, avocado.

Balanced Life

Keto Power

  • Breakfast: Bacon, eggs, and avocado.
  • Lunch: Caesar salad with extra chicken (no croutons).
  • Dinner: Ribeye steak with asparagus butter.

Plant Strength

  • Breakfast: Tofu scramble with spinach and toast.

  • Dinner: Chickpea curry with brown rice.
